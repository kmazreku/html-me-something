What is the new Normal?

Coronavirus has changed how we think, behave and connect to one another. Some of these changes have been more subtle while others have forcefully been pushed on us transforming our day-to-day life to our new normal. We never imagined a reality where world economy would be ruined in a blink of an eye because of a virus or that families won’t be allowed to go outside in fear of getting infected.

Some family are financially struggling trying to figure out how to feed everyone and how to avoid eviction. While the lucky working parents are trying to juggle the new responsibility of working from home and on the same time are teaching and entertaining their kids. For others this has been a great opportunity to slow down, reconnect and spend time with their family.

We all know that after COVID-19 everyday life as we know it won’t be the same and we are yet to find what the new 'Normal' will look for us but in the mean time we can try to take this one day-at-a-time and make the best of it.

Getting the Cabin Fever or Kids Are Driving You Crazy?

Read a few of other parents post and laugh a bit. Life is to short to take yourself too seriously.

Set a virtual happy hour or a movie watch party with your besties. You can even treat yourself with your favorite adult beverage. You know it is 5 o’clock somewhere and if the family is bothering you could mention that they will be cleaning the whole house once you are done with the party. I bet they won’t bother you after this.

Need to regain some sanity? Visit one of the beautiful Nature Preserves in Tampa Bay and don’t forget 6ft apart distancing rule.
